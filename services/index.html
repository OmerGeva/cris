Services

Collector Advisory

For individuals and entities building and managing nascent and mature collections, as well as for those seeking innovative approaches to refining their collection.

Artist Advisory

For living artists and artists' estates, with services complementary to existing galleries and professional support teams, with a special emphasis on legacy planning and implementation.

Museum Advisory

These services assist institutions in adapting to the evolving cultural landscape. Whether for established or emerging museums, I provide strategic guidance on defining identities, fulfilling missions, and engaging communities. My expertise includes spatial planning, organizational evaluation, strategic development, fundraising, and collection management, all aimed at supporting long-term success and sustainability.

Philanthropic Advisory

Personalized philanthropic advisory services designed to create impactful and sustainable giving strategies. Whether working with individual patrons or organizations, I help develop a philanthropic philosophy that aligns with their values. My approach includes thematic, artist-focused, and collection-based programs, as well as guidance on legacies and strategic planning to maximize impact.
